Our Story

Built by developers, for developers

We started Nexus because we were frustrated with the complexity of modern tooling. We believed there had to be a better way to build, deploy, and scale applications.

Today, Nexus powers thousands of teams across the globe, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our platform handles billions of requests daily while maintaining the simplicity that made us popular in the first place.

We're backed by leading investors and are growing fast, but we've never lost sight of our core mission: make building software delightful.
